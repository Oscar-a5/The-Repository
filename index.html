Small Farm Tourist Attraction

The farm, nestled in the scenic countryside between Edinburgh and Carlisle, has been thoughtfully transformed into a vibrant and family-friendly visitor attraction. In addition to its charming shop offering a wide variety of locally sourced produce and plants, visitors can enjoy an intimate petting zoo where they can interact with friendly animals. The onsite cafe provides a cozy spot to relax and sample homemade treats or freshly brewed coffee, with a focus on local ingredients. For families, the farm features a well-maintained play park that offers children a chance to run, climb, and explore in a safe and enjoyable environment. Nature lovers will appreciate the various walking trails that wind through the surrounding fields, providing stunning views of the landscape and a peaceful escape into nature. Seasonal events, workshops, and educational programs also take place regularly, making it a perfect destination for day trips, school visits, and community gatherings. The farm’s commitment to sustainability and supporting local agriculture is evident throughout, making it a destination that is both fun and educational.
