Petting Zoo

Chicken

A curious, fluffy chicken with bright feathers pecks at the ground, searching for seeds and bugs. It isn’t shy, allowing visitors to gently pet its soft plumage while clucking quietly.

Sheep

A gentle, fluffy sheep with a thick wool coat calmly grazes in the petting zoo, occasionally lifting its head to nibble on the grass. Visitors can approach and pet its soft wool, enjoying its quiet, peaceful nature.

Pig

A friendly, small pig with a pink, wrinkled snout and soft, bristly fur happily roams around the petting zoo. It enjoys the attention of visitors, letting them pet and feed it while snuffling through the straw.

Cow

A calm, large cow with a smooth coat stands in the petting zoo, gently grazing on the grass. Visitors can approach to pet its soft, warm fur, as it gazes around contentedly in the peaceful environment.
